About Pan Asia

Pan Asian Brands, formerly known as Brewbakes Group Enterprises; established in 2014 is a well-recognized and pioneer of the hospitality industry, popularised for the concepts of casual and fine dining and QSR. Followed by its success, Chaat Ka Chaska; for casual dining and QSR, The American Shakes; QSR milkshakes, The Idli Xpress; for casual dining and QSR were emerged. Besides, The Chopstick Buddies; for casual dining and QSR and Burger Worger; for QSR are also best-selling franchise brands. The new addition to the team is Amchoor- casual dining and QSR. We have healthy alternatives in the form of GO Juice! Additionally, the Studio99 salons is one of our health and wellness brand. We are not staying behind in terms of education; Madhu Academy and Studio99 Academy are among them.
